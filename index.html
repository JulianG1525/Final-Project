About us

Jeetar, founded in 2025 by Julian Gamet, built up by musicians and music lovers, was created to rebuild the idea that music unifies the world. Our knowledge hopes to bring musicians together to create and connect to help and heal the world. So, follow us and find your sound.

Our Values:

  • Highest quality gear for all musicians
  • Consistency and trust
  • Customer Satisfaction
  • Community involvement
  • Most importantly, passion and curiosity

Why should you shop here?

At Jeetar, we believe that every musician deserves access to high-quality instruments and gear that inspire creativity and elevate their sound. Our curated selection of guitars, amplifiers, and accessories is handpicked by industry experts to ensure that you receive only the best products on the market.
